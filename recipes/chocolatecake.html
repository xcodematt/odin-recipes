Vegan Chocolate Cake

Description

This is the best vegan chocolate cake that you will ever try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup soy milk

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Bake at 180 degrees.
  3. Eat with your friends and family.
